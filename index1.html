About TECHNIX 7.0

BMS Institute of Technology & Management takes pride in educating students since 1946 in various fields of Engineering and continues to provide world class education with more emphasis on research and development.

The Department of MCA at BMSIT&M is a two-year full-time postgraduate course affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University. Established in 2003, it has an intake of 120 students and is accredited by NBA twice. The department has obtained 13 ranks at VTU level since inception.
